# Purpose

This document defines the sponsorship strategy for Night Gym Academy.

The goal is to build long-term partnerships with trusted brands that support education and the fitness industry.

Sponsors should enhance the learning experience without compromising educational quality.

---

# Sponsorship Vision

Create partnerships with the world's leading fitness brands while maintaining the independence and credibility of Night Gym Academy.

---

# Sponsorship Rules

Sponsors must respect the educational mission of Night Gym Academy.

Sponsors cannot influence scientific content.

Educational accuracy always comes before commercial interests.

No misleading advertisements.

No false claims.

No unsafe products.
