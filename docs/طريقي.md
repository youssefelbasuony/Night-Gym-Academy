# Vision

Build the world's most comprehensive fitness education platform that helps students, coaches, athletes, and healthcare professionals learn fitness science through high-quality education, practical tools, and modern technology.

---

# Mission

Create a professional educational ecosystem that combines:

- Fitness Science
- Anatomy
- Nutrition
- Exercise Library
- Exams
- AI
- Certifications

inside one modern platform.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Night Gym Academy is not just a website.

It is a complete educational ecosystem under **YFLB** designed to become one of the world's leading fitness education platforms.
