# Purpose

This document defines the business strategy for Night Gym Academy.

The objective is to build a sustainable educational business under YFLB while delivering high-quality fitness education.

---

# Parent Company

YFLB

YFLB is the parent company responsible for developing educational, technology, and digital products.

Night Gym Academy is the first official product under YFLB.

Future projects will also operate under YFLB.

---

# Vision

Become one of the world's leading fitness education platforms.

Create an international educational ecosystem that combines science, technology, and modern learning.
