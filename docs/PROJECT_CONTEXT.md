# Project Overview

Night Gym Academy is a premium educational platform focused on fitness science.

The goal is not to create a simple course website.

The goal is to build one of the world's best educational platforms for fitness professionals.

The platform is designed for long-term growth and international expansion.

---

# Parent Company

YFLB

Night Gym Academy is the first major product under YFLB.

Future products will also be developed under YFLB.

---

# Vision

Build the world's most comprehensive fitness education platform.

Provide professional education for students, coaches, athletes, and healthcare professionals.

Become an internationally recognized learning platform.

---

# Mission

Deliver structured, scientific, practical, and easy-to-understand fitness education.

Focus on education quality before monetization.

Create a platform that helps people learn correctly instead of simply consuming information.

---
